Welcome to SkyLine.

SkyLine is an app that allows you to put your schedule into the upcoming weather week so that it can show you a animation of what your upcoming day/week will look like. It will have the standard weather and temperature but it will also suggest clothing choices you should make, have tips on if you should leave 10 mins early to miss the rain. You can put in your hobbies and favourite clothing peices so it will alert you if it's a good day to go swimming, or to wear those thick leather pants you love.

We want to help you to visualize what your day is going to be like, so if your little cartoon version of yourself is wearing a raincoat at 5, you will think about taking a raincoat with you to work. Rather than have the information but not take into account that you will be walking home at 5 when it is scheduled to rain.

This app helps you to visualise your week.
